Cooperation and commercialization of technologies

What is this article about?​

Suppose that

  • you have made some product or technology yourself (drone, EW/ERP device, navigation system) that helps our defense forces destroy the occupiers, but you do not have the ability to scale production and would like to join your efforts with some more powerful manufacturer.
  • or you have an idea for some component (inertial navigation system, target tracking, rocket launcher for drones) that will be useful to many other manufacturers, but you don't know under what conditions you could offer your development.

In this article, we present possible types of cooperation and conditions for sharing responsibility and profit, mechanisms for interaction with partners, and measures to protect one's own rights.
You will also learn about additional ways to commercialize development rights with the necessary investment, technology and capabilities
