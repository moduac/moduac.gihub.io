Frequently asked questions

1. Why was the Ministry of Defense Accelerator created when Brave 1 already exists?

These two structures complement each other. Brave1 is a startup fund where you can submit your project at an early stage and where they can help you with financing and turning your idea into a finished product. While the AMO team is engaged in the optimization of internal processes of the Ministry of Defense, and helps manufacturers of ready-made samples of OVT (TRL 7 or higher) as soon as possible to obtain permission for operation and start supplying their products to the troops.
